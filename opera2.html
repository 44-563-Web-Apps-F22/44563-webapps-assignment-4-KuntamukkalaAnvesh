JUP.

What fools to give themselves so much exertion

DIA.

A government survey I'll make assertion.

AP.

Perhaps the Alpine clubs their diversion.

MER.

They seem to be more like a "Cook's" excursion.

ALL.

Goodness gracious, etc.

AP.

If, mighty Jove, you value your existence, Send them a thunderbolt with your regards.

CHO.

Through the night, the constellations,

JUP.

My thunderbolts, though valid at a distance, Are not effective at a hundred yards.

MER.

Let the moon's rays, Diana, strike 'em flighty, Make 'em all lunatics in various styles.

DIA.

My lunar rays unhappily are mighty Only at many hundred thousand miles.

ALL.

Goodness gracious, etc...

[Exeunt Jupiter, Apollo, Diana, and Mercury into ruined temple]

[Enter Sparkeion and Nicemis climbing mountain at back.]
